Welcome to UO Supermileage

The Forefront of Electric Car Racing at uOttawa

  • Join Our Team

  • The only electric car team at the University of Ottawa.

    UO Supermileage is the only team in the University of Ottawa that design and create an electric vehicle to compete against the world. Our team members work on multiple aspects of the car, from mechanical, electrical and software.

  • 4th Place in 2019 Americas Shell Eco-Marathon

    Last April, our team competed in the Shell Eco-Marathon Americas 2019, where our car placed 4th overall in the electric prototype category in all of Americas! Our hard work throughout the year and the sponsors that supported us allowed our team to secure such a feat.

  • Over 100 members strong within uOttawa

    Our team started small in 2016 with a measly 10 members. Over our two years UO Supermileage has grown to have over 100 members and continues to grow and improve everyday.

  • Opportunities for Mentorship and Learning

    Work alongside experienced students who are at the top of their respective engineering domains.
